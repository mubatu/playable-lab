### **Game Overview**
* **Title:** Block Blast!
* **Genre:** Casual Puzzle
* **Platform:** Mobile (iOS / Android)
* **Resolution:** 1080x1920
* **Logline:** A hyper-casual block puzzle game where players strategically place colorful shapes onto a grid to clear lines, and achieve the highest score possible before running out of space.

---

### **Core Gameplay Loop**
1.  **Evaluate:** The player looks at the 8x8 (configurable) board and the 3 (configurable) available block shapes at the bottom of the screen.
2.  **Place:** The player drags and drops a shape onto the board. 
3.  **Clear & Score:** If the placement completes a horizontal or vertical line, the line clears, awarding points.
4.  **Repeat:** Once all 3 shapes are used, a new set of 3 appears. The loop continues until there is no valid space left for the given shapes.

---

### **Mechanics**
* **The Grid:** An 8x8 (configurable) board. Unlike Tetris, there is no gravity; blocks stay exactly where they are placed.
* **The Blocks:** Shapes made of connected squares (similar to Tetrominoes and Pentominoes). Players cannot rotate the blocks.
* **Spawn System:** The game provides blocks in sets of three (configurable). The player can place them in any order, but all three must be placed before a new set is generated.
* **Line Clearing:** Filling a complete row or column (8 blocks) destroys those blocks, opening up space.
* **Fail State:** The game ends immediately when the player is unable to fit any of their currently available pieces onto the board. 

---

### **Art Direction**
* **Visuals:** Clean, flat, and vibrant 2D UI. The background is typically dark to make the brightly colored blocks "pop" off the screen. 
* **Game Feel (Juice):** Placing blocks should feel tactile. Clearing lines should trigger satisfying particle effects and screen shake, emphasizing the "Blast."

---

### **Progression**
* **Progression:** There are no levels to beat. The primary motivation is intrinsically tied to beating personal high scores.
